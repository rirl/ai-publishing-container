== The God Field and the Ontological Disjunction

To integrate this personal baseline with contemporary scientific and cosmological data, the framework establishes a dedicated metaphysical repository: *The God Field*. The God Field is the fundamental, non-physical environment of personal consciousness that contains, orders, and continuously sustains all physical reality. God is not merely one more heavy object floating around _inside_ the universe; rather, the material cosmos exists _within_ the active, operational presence of the God Field. Labeled as a *Universal Baseline Meta*, the field redefines our understanding of natural laws. The laws of physics—such as gravity, electromagnetism, and thermodynamics—are not self-existent, eternal rules ruling over dead matter. They are the consistent, regular habits of the God Field. If the Creator’s relational intent were to switch off for a single millisecond, the physical universe would instantly collapse back into absolute nothingness (cf. Colossians 1:17).

Crucially, to survive intense academic peer review, this model maintains a strict *creator-creation disjunction* to completely avoid the errors of panentheism (the idea that the universe is contained within the literal being of God, or that God's essence pervades the cosmos).

[NOTE]
=====================================================================
God does not share His uncreated essence (_ousia_) with the physical universe, nor does the cosmos emanate naturally from His being like heat from a fire. The Triune God is entirely self-sufficient, completely transcendent, and ontologically distinct from everything He has made. The term "God Field" is an instrumental, metaphorical model representing God’s operative, preserving, and omnipresent governance—not an ontological description of His essence. The universe exists within the covenantal care of the God Field, but it remains strictly creaturely, maintaining an ironclad boundary between the independent, uncreated Creator and the dependent, created universe.
=====================================================================
